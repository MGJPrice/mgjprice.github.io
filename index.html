About Me

PhD Student at Lancaster University, Network Research Group

My work focuses on modern ways to map the external attack surface of modern organisations, both public bodies and private corporations. This work forms the basis of my PhD Thesis, and has been presented at a variety of venues, such as SIGCOMM and the Next Generation Networking, Multi-Service Networks workshop in the UK.

Alongside this academic work, I also have a personal interest in the cyber security of space systems. This fascination led to my first class MSc Cyber Security Dissertation wherein I created a novel Intrusion Detection System for a simulated version of ESA's OPS-SAT.
