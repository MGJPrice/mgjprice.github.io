About Me

PhD Student at Lancaster University, Network Research Group

My work focuses on novel approaches to map the external attack surface of modern organisations, for both public bodies and private corporations. This research forms the basis of my PhD Thesis, and has been presented at a variety of venues, such as SIGCOMM, RIPE, and the Next Generation Networking, Multi-Service Networks workshop in the UK.

Alongside this academic work, I also have a personal interest in the cyber security of space systems. This fascination led to my distinction grade MSc Cyber Security Dissertation wherein I created a novel Intrusion Detection System for a simulated version of ESA's OPS-SAT.
